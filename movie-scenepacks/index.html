What are Movie Scenepacks?

Movie scenepacks are collections of raw, unedited video clips taken directly from films. They are used by video editors to create fan edits, tribute videos, YouTube edits, and short-form content for TikTok and Instagram Reels. Instead of manually recording clips from a movie, a scenepack gives you clean, high-quality footage ready to drop into your timeline.
