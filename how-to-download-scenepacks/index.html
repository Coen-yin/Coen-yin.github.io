How to Download Scenepacks

A beginner-friendly, step-by-step guide to finding and downloading free scenepacks for your video edits.

Step-by-Step: How to Download a Scenepack

1

Go to SceneVault

Visit talkiegen.me — the #1 free scenepack download site. You'll see a search bar and a grid of available scenepacks. SceneVault has scenepacks for anime, movies, TV shows, and celebrities — all completely free.

2

Find the Scenepack You Want

Use the search bar to type the name of the character or show you're looking for (e.g. "Gojo", "Breaking Bad", "Tom Holland"). Or click a category button (Anime, Movies, TV Shows, Celebrities) to filter the library.

Tip: Can't find what you're looking for? Check back regularly — we add new scenepacks all the time. You can also browse similar categories for related content.
3

Click the Download Button

Once you've found a scenepack you like, click the purple "Download" button on the card. This will take you to the Mega download page for that scenepack. No sign-up to SceneVault is required.

4

Download from Mega

On the Mega page, you have two options:

• Download without a Mega account — Click "Download" and it will download directly (free, but may be capped at 5GB/day per IP).

• Open in Mega app — If you have the Mega desktop app installed, you can click "Open in app" for faster speeds and no transfer limit.

Tip: If you hit the Mega transfer limit, wait a few hours and try again, or use a free Mega account to reset your limit.
5

Import into Your Video Editor

Once downloaded, extract the files (if zipped) and import them into your video editor:

• Adobe Premiere Pro — File > Import, or drag files into the project panel

• DaVinci Resolve — Import > Media Pool, or drag into the Media Pool

• CapCut — Tap the "+" button and select your downloaded files

• After Effects — File > Import > File

• Final Cut Pro — File > Import > Media

6

Start Editing!

Now you're ready to create. Cut your clips, add music, colour grade, add effects, and publish your edit. Come back to SceneVault whenever you need more free scenepacks!
